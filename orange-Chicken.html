Orange Chicken Recipe

Juicy, flavorfull and tender orange chicken with a 20 minute preparation, the best side dish to go with rice.

Ingridients

  • boneless chicken thighs
  • Cornstarch
  • flour
  • garlic
  • 1 egg
  • 2 Oranges
  • salt
  • pepper
  • soy sauce
  • ginger
  • Sugar>
  • Rice Vinegar
  • Vegetable Oil

Instructions

How to make the chicken

  1. Put a wok with oil to mid-high heat
  2. For the batter,in a bowl mix flour, egg, salt, pepper and water. Then in another bowl set the cornstarch
  3. Coat the chicken in the cornstarch and then pass trough the batter
  4. Deep fry the chicken in two to three batches
  5. After frying remove the wok/pan from the heat end carefuly discar the oil.

How to make the sauce

  1. In the wok/pan add the juice of the oranges and a bit of the zest, then add sugar, ginger, garlic, soy sauce and the rice vinegar
  2. put the wok in mid-high and let it bubble up till it thickend and turn the heat off
  3. add the chicken bac in to the wok and toss it till is all coated
  4. Finally side it with rice and enjoy
