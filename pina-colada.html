Piña Colada Recipe

An easy and delicious bevarage to enjoy on summer with friends

Ingridients

  • 6 ounces white rum
  • 6 ounces cream of coconut
  • 6 ounces pineapple juice
  • 1/2 cup frozen pineapple chunks
  • 4 cups ice
  • 4 ounces golden or añejo rum

Instructions

  1. Place the white rum, coconut cream, pineapple juice and chunks, and ice in a blender. Process until totally blended.
  2. Pour into glasses and top with and top with the golden rum (it should float on top)
  3. Serve with pineapple leaves or slices as garnish and serve immediately.
