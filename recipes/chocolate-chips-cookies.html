Chocolate chip cookies

An easy and delicious chocolate chip recipe to share with your beloved

ones while enjoy a good time baking and eating a quick dessert.

These are some bakery worth cookies, easy to do and friendly for beginners. Soft, sweet and irressistible. Keep out of the kids sigth... Or there will be nothing left for you.

Ingridients

  • Butter
  • Vanilla
  • Baking Soda
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Flour
  • Chocolate chips

Step-By-Step Process

  1. Beat the butter and sugars, then beat in the eggs and vanilla.
  2. Dissolve the baking soda in hot water and add to the mixture.
  3. Stir in the flour, chocolate chips, and walnuts.
  4. Drop dough onto a prepared baking sheet.
  5. Bake until the edges are golden brown.
